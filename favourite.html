My favourite song

Locksmith-Sadie Jean

"Locksmith" by Sadie Jean is a song about a person who has moved on from a past relationship, but still holds a strong emotional attachment to their former partner. The lyrics suggest that the singer has accepted that the relationship is over and that they need to let go, but they also acknowledge that their feelings for the other person are still present.

My favourite travel destination

Makkah

Mecca is the holiest of Muslim cities. Muhammad, the founder of Islam, was born in Mecca, and it is toward this Kaabah that Muslims turn five times daily in prayer. All devout and able Muslims attempt a hajj (pilgrimage) to Mecca at least once in their lifetime. And because it is sacred, only Muslims are allowed to enter the city. My favourite sport

Badminton

Badminton is a court or lawn game played with lightweight rackets and a shuttlecock, with several rules . The game was first played around 1873 at Badminton, the country estate of the dukes of Beaufort in Gloucestershire, England. The roots of the sport can be traced to ancient Greece, China, and India. It is played by an estimated 220 million people worldwide, with the sport becoming more popular each year.
